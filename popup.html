Upload Booking Reservations XLS 1. Download reservations list from Booking in csv form (filter:Hotel Collect(optional)).
2. Upload the XLS file here and when its processed run the bot.
3. The bot will check reservation statuses in Choice Advantage.
4. “Reservation Status” will show In House, Cancelled, or No Show. 5. By default we only show the cancelled and No show filters.
